# Fantasy Species

In the Fantasy setting, your species determines your innate capabilities and cultural background. Choose one species during Step 5 (Traits) of Character Creation.

## Human
Humans are adaptable, ambitious, and diverse. They can be found in every corner of the world, from bustling cities to isolated frontiers.

*   **Attribute Increase**: Increase two different Attributes by **+1**.
*   **Versatile**: You gain proficiency in **one additional Skill** of your choice.
*   **Determined**: Once per day, when you fail a Core Check, you can reroll it. You must use the new result.

## Elf
Elves are a long-lived people of magical grace and connection to nature. They often live in secluded forests or glittering spires.

*   **Attribute Increase**: Increase **Agility** by **+1** and **Intellect** by **+1**.
*   **Keen Senses**: You are Trained in the **Insight** skill (or **Investigation** if already trained).
*   **Fey Ancestry**: You have **Advantage** on Defense Checks against being charmed, and magic cannot put you to sleep.
*   **Trance**: Elves do not sleep. Instead, they meditate deeply for 4 hours a day. After resting in this way, you gain the same benefit that a human does from 8 hours of sleep.

## Dwarf
Dwarves are solid and enduring like the mountains they love. They are master craftsmen, warriors, and miners.

*   **Attribute Increase**: Increase **Might** by **+1** and **Will** by **+1**.
*   **Dwarven Resilience**: You have **Advantage** on Defense Checks against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage.
*   **Stonecunning**: You have **Advantage** on **Lore** checks related to the origin of stonework.
*   **Toughness**: Your **Vitality** maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level.
